# DevAtlas Vision

## The Problem

Iranian developers face unique technical challenges that most global platforms ignore:

1. **Sanctions & Service Restrictions**: Many developer tools, APIs, cloud services, and documentation platforms are partially or fully blocked
2. **Internet Instability**: Frequent disruptions, throttling, and filtering make real-time dependency on external services unreliable
3. **Knowledge Fragmentation**: Critical technical knowledge is scattered across Telegram channels, personal blogs, and ephemeral social media posts — with no structured, searchable, reliable source

These are not political problems. They are **engineering constraints** that require engineering solutions.

---

## The Solution

DevAtlas is a **technical knowledge platform** purpose-built for resilience. It provides:

### Pillar 1: Structured Technical Content

- Curated developer guides organized by topic, difficulty, and relationships
- Tool directory with alternatives and comparisons
- AI resource catalog (models, frameworks, datasets)
- All content stored as MDX with typed frontmatter schemas

### Pillar 2: Resilient Infrastructure Services

- **Offline-first architecture**: content works without constant connectivity
- **Self-hostable**: entire platform can run on local infrastructure
- **Semantic search**: AI-powered search that works on local embeddings
- **Knowledge graph**: content relationships enable discovery beyond keyword matching

---

## Target Audience

1. **Iranian software engineers** who need reliable access to structured technical knowledge
2. **Development teams** who want a self-hostable knowledge base
3. **Any developer** in restricted-internet environments worldwide

---

## Design Principles

| Principle | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Resilience over convenience** | Every feature must work under network constraints |
| **Structure over volume** | 100 well-organized guides > 10,000 scattered posts |
| **Local-first** | Core functionality must not depend on external APIs |
| **Open and self-hostable** | No vendor lock-in, no proprietary dependencies |
| **Progressive enhancement** | Basic content works everywhere; AI/search enhance when available |

---

## What DevAtlas Is NOT

- Not a blog platform
- Not a social network
- Not a tutorial aggregator
- Not a replacement for official documentation

DevAtlas is a **structured, searchable, resilient knowledge system** for developers who cannot rely on always-available internet or unrestricted access to global services.
